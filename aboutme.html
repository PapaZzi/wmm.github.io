About Us

Who are we ?

We are a group of 4 students who come from Malaysia and China that is very enthusiastic about taking picture. Our main photo object is mostly is figures from all kind of species, which includes toy, anime figures, decoration and so on. We created this portal to increase anime's influence by allowing people to learn about and watch anime figures.




History

Our toy photography website was created on February 7, 2022 by Wan YiXuan , Ding YiChen , Kelvin Tan Yin Kang and Kelvin Cheong.



Our mission

Post some photos about the exquisite figure,entice some like-minded or curious users to visit our website and take a look.
