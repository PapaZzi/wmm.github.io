Surtr

Surtr is a fictional character from Chinese Gaming: Arknights. Arknights is a free-to-play tactical RPG and tower defense mobile game developed by Chinese developer Hypergryph. It was released in China on 1 May 2019, in other countries on 16 January 2020. Arknights is available on Android and iOS platforms and features gacha game mechanics.
