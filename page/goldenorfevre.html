Golden Orfevre

Orfevre is a retired Japanese Thoroughbred racehorse and active breeding stallion. He started his career in 2010 and in 2011 he won the Japanese Triple Crown and was voted Japanese Horse of the Year. In 2013 Orfevre ending his career with an eight length win.

Interestingly, Orfevre has the same father as the golden ship . So Orfevre and the golden ship are brother. Like the golden ship, it also has two different types'doll.
