Golden Ship

Gold Ship is a Japanese Thoroughbred racehorse. In a racing career which began in July 2011, he ran twenty-eight times and won thirteen races. He was known for his unpredictable temperament, with major wins being interspersed with inexplicably poor efforts.

Due to its excellent performance and interesting character, the official soon launched two dolls. One is sitting and the other is lying. These two dolls are very popular among horse racing fans
