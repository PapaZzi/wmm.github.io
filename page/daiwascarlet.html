Dawai Scarlet

Dawai Scarlet was born in Hokkaido on May 13, 2004. It is a rare mare in horse racing. Started career in 2007. In 2008, Dawai Scarlet retired due to injury after winning the horse championship. Career calculation 12 wars, 8 wins and 4 losses. Be known as Crimson Meteors.

As a mare, Daiwa Scarlet can achieve remarkable results so that it has a great reputation and a large number of fans in Japan. So Daiwa Scarlet also has two different types'doll.
